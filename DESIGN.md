# Saras Totey Portfolio Design System

## Concept

The home page is a sakura flight through the things Saras actually shipped: cinematic landscapes carry the emotion; concise editorial overlays carry the evidence.

## Typography

- Display: Boska, a high-contrast serif that gives personal statements and project theses an authored voice.
- Body: Switzer, a precise but warm grotesk for long technical explanations.
- Utility/data: JetBrains Mono for citations, counters, system labels, and verified metrics.

## Color

- Night ink `#17182B`: quantitative pages and technical depth.
- Sakura paper `#F7E8ED`: world overlays and project canvases.
- Sakura accent `#D64B83`: the single signature action color.
- Plum ink `#251C26`: readable foreground on light scenes.
- Rice paper `#F4EBDD`: warm contrast and body copy.

## Layout and motion

- Full-bleed imagery; UI never competes with the world.
- Alternating left, right, and centered text placement creates chapter rhythm.
- One signature motion: scroll scrubs a continuous camera chain, with a single reveal per chapter boundary.
- Reduced-motion mode replaces scrubbing with static scene posters.

## Anti-patterns

- No generic centered SaaS hero, gradient text, purple-pink gradients, floating card grids, emoji icons, or decorative metrics.
- Glass is reserved for controls over moving imagery; content surfaces remain editorial and flat.
- Buttons use one accent and clear verbs.

## Reference

Apple’s image-first restraint, adapted to Saras’s sakura world and technical/editorial framing. Taste references: wesleyslaw.com, waabi.ai, sunday.ai, and forkd.site.
